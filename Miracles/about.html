About us

Our Organization

Our Services

We are forward to provide services to these needy children.If miracles could happen then we should participate in efforts to make it happen for sake of humanity.We accelerate hopes for children suffering from cancer and their families by raising a public fund through physical events and through donations made on line.We serve humanity because humanity serves soul of being human itself.

Our Mission

Our Objective is to compensate the disability of being a cancer patient on children so that they can reincarnate their lost childhood and smile on their parent's face.Every victim have a right to live their life like other children.The enigmas raised due to such dilemma need to be reduced and our efforts and devotion for service of these kids can really bring a change and will change their life.

Our Alliances

We don't need any alliances for humanity.Everyone can contribute for the welfare of children suffering from the cancer.Maybe Cancer is incurable but the warmth of humanity can heal the bruise left behind by the deadly Cancer and its agents on our little aspiring and growing kids.The future of every kid on this planet must be guaranteed to regulate peace and joyous ambience where humanity prevails.
