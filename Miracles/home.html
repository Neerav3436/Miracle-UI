Our Events & Activities

Cancer Awareness Campaign

We are planning to organise an event to support cancer suffering children to remark them that our community is always with them. The objective of event will be to spread a motivation and passion among people for service of such kids who may not be privileged by god but by the society.

Meet the experts

If you know somebody suffering from such carcinogenic ailment bring them to our experts who will be happy to provide them counselling for free.Also the doctors will be providing a session on classes of cancers and prospective symptoms as well as possible treatments.

National Childhood Cancer Awareness Day

This September 13 we are celebrating event to help those children with cancer.Our miracle foundation is going to organise a get together and talk with the guardians of these children and find out some way to help them and give them a light of hope securing future of their kids. we are going to organise games to stress out these kids.

Extension of our NGO

We are expanding.We are opening a new office in down town to make it more accessible to public and improve fund raising. We hope that public would like our new infrastructure.

Our Vision
